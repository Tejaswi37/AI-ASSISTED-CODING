Secure Auth
A zero-server biometric authentication system powered by MediaPipe Hand Landmark detection. Register hand gestures, verify identities, and analyze accuracy metrics — entirely in your browser.
🤚
21 Landmark Detection
MediaPipe extracts 21 3D keypoints per hand frame for high-fidelity gesture fingerprinting.
📐
Cosine Similarity
Normalized landmark vectors compared via cosine distance for rotation-invariant matching.
